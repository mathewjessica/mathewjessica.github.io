Website Evaluations

Good Website

Apple.com is a website showcasing Apple's products and services with a focus on innovation and design.

  • Contrast: The site uses bold visuals and ample white space to make text and products stand out clearly.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, colors, and layouts unify the design and reinforce Appleâ€™s branding.
  • Alignment: Perfect alignment of text, images, and buttons creates a clean and professional layout.
  • Proximity: Related elements, like product images and descriptions, are grouped logically for easy navigation.

Bad Website

Suzanne Collins' official website, suzannecollinsbooks.com, serves as a platform to showcase her literary works and provide updates to fans.

  • Contrast: The website's use of low-contrast text against similar backgrounds makes reading difficult, reducing overall readability.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent font styles and varying design elements across pages disrupt visual harmony and weaken brand identity.
  • Alignment: Misaligned text and images create a disorganized appearance, detracting from the site's professionalism.
  • Proximity: Poor spacing between related content leads to a cluttered layout, making it challenging for users to navigate and locate information efficiently.
