Project Description

The Catholic Campus Ministry (CCM) at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte serves as a spiritual hub for students looking to grow in their faith, participate in service opportunities, and build a supportive community. The goal of this web application is to provide information about CCM, including its mission, events, leadership, and opportunities to get involved.

Intended Users of the Website

The primary users of this website are UNC Charlotte students who are either members of the Catholic Campus Ministry or those interested in learning more about CCM and getting involved. The website will also serve the staff and faculty who support the ministry and wish to stay updated on events and activities.

Page Design Overview

Home Page

  • Purpose: Introduce the CCM and provide links to other pages.
  • Audience: All visitors to the site, especially prospective members.
  • Content: Introduction, upcoming events, and links to other pages.
  • Interactivity: Links to other pages, potential forms for registration or sign-ups.

About Page

  • Purpose: Provide information about the mission and history of CCM.
  • Audience: Prospective members and donors.
  • Content: Mission statement, history, and core values of CCM.
  • Interactivity: Links to social media profiles or donation pages.

Leadership Page

  • Purpose: Introduce the leadership team and their roles in CCM.
  • Audience: All members of the community who want to learn more about the leadership.
  • Content: Photos and short bios of leadership members.
  • Interactivity: Optional: Hover effects or clickable profiles for more information.

Events Page

  • Purpose: Display upcoming events for the CCM community.
  • Audience: Current and prospective members of CCM.
  • Content: Event descriptions, dates, times, and registration options.
  • Interactivity: Event registration forms, links to external event pages.

Gallery Page

  • Purpose: Display photos from past events.
  • Audience: Anyone wanting to see what CCM activities look like.
  • Content: Image gallery with descriptions or captions.
  • Interactivity: Lightbox feature or hover effects for viewing images in full screen.

Dynamic Functionality

The website will implement the following dynamic features:

  • Event Registration Forms: A form to collect user details for upcoming events.
  • Interactive Gallery: A JavaScript-powered image gallery where users can view images in full-screen mode.
  • Interactive Maps: A map showing the location of CCM events and services using a Google Maps API.
  • AJAX: Use of AJAX to dynamically load new events or content without reloading the page.
