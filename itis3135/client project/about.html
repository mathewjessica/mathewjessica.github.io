About Us

Our Mission

At the UNC Charlotte Catholic Campus Ministry (CCM), we are a vibrant community of students passionate about living out our Catholic faith. Our ministry is a place where students from diverse backgrounds come together to grow in their spiritual journey, build lasting friendships, and serve the greater community.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to create a welcoming, supportive, and dynamic environment where students can explore and deepen their faith, all while making meaningful connections with others who share the same values. Whether you're a lifelong Catholic or someone looking to explore your faith, CCM is a place where you can belong.

Through regular Mass, Bible studies, retreats, volunteer opportunities, and fellowship events, we offer numerous ways for students to engage, connect, and grow. Our community is committed to fostering an inclusive space where everyone feels welcomed, loved, and encouraged to pursue a deeper relationship with God.

At CCM, you’ll find more than just a ministry; you’ll find a family of students dedicated to supporting each other, both in faith and in life. We invite you to join us, get involved, and be part of a transformative experience that will enrich your time at UNC Charlotte.
